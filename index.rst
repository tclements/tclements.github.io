I am a 4th year PhD student in the `Earthquake Seismology group <https://quake.fas.harvard.edu/>`_ in Earth and Planetary Sciences at Harvard University, advised by Prof. Marine Denolle. I have interests in environmental seismology, numeri\
cal methods, machine learning and cloud computing. My research focuses on using the Earth's ambient wavefield to monitor changes in soil moisture and groundwater levels.

I am a Co-Chair of `HGWISE Guys <https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/hgwise/hgwise-guys>`_ at Harvard. HGWISE Guys is an extension of HGWISE (Harvard Graduate Women in Science and Engineering) where all Harvard graduate students, regardless of their gender, can engage in issues of gender, bias and harassment in science.
